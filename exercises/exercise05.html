Amor Alien

Amor Alien is a painting by latinx visual artist Laura Molina. This painting was created using oil paint, fluorescent enamel and metallic powder on canvas.
It was completed in 2004 and was displayed at the National Museum of Mexican Art in their permanent collection. In 2019 it was displayed a the Queens Museum in New York for their exhibition called Mundos Alternos.
