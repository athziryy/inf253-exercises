Once Upon a Time... In Hollywood

Once Upon a Time... In Hollywood is the ninth film by director Quentin Tarantino and stars Leonardo DiCaprio, Brad Pitt, and Margot Robbie.
The film takes place in 1969 with changes not only in society and youth culture but also in the film industry. With multiple storylines it follows
the story of a fading actor, his stunt double, and the looming threat of the Manson family. It is loosely based off the 1969 Tate-LaBianca murders.
